Physical fitness is the key to mental well-being and a healthier life. Reducing the gap between you and your healthy self might pose a problem with traveling, expenses, and pandemics coming up as hurdles between you and your trainer. This application is aimed at providing perfect guidance for any individual who wants to learn exercises all by himself with instruction provided at every step throughout the course. The application filters exercises according to different criteria required to complete them efficiently. The app will provide commands in a step-by-step fashion leading to the trainee completing the exercise. In case the trainee ends up in a wrong posture, the app will notify him, using computer vision analysis.
